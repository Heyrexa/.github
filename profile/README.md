## 🚀 About

Heyrexa is an AI assistant platform designed to help individuals and businesses automate their workflows using intelligent AI agents.

Our mission is to make powerful AI accessible through seamless integrations, automation, and developer-friendly tools.

- 📁 Google Drive
- 🔎 Web Search
